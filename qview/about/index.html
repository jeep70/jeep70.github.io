About qView

On Windows, I had grown quite accustomed to using pViewer as my image viewer of choice. After I started using Linux more often, I found myself missing pViewer more than I had expected. I set out to make an open source Qt-based alternative to pViewer, allowing me to view my images with minimal GUI, simple controls, and on any operating system I chose. After about five months of development, I hope it has been worth the wait. I hope you can find yourself enjoying qView as much as I do!

Thank you for using qView.
